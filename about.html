About IHRS

The International Human Rights Society (IHRS) is a student-led community at our university dedicated to promoting human rights awareness, education, and advocacy. Our members organize events, campaigns, and seminars to engage with local and global human rights issues.

Our Mission

  • Raise awareness about human rights globally and locally.
  • Organize educational seminars, campaigns, and discussions.
  • Collaborate with NGOs, student groups, and international organizations.
  • Provide a platform for students to advocate for social justice.
