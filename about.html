About IHRS

The International Human Rights Society (IHRS) is a student-led community committed to promoting awareness, advocacy, and education on human rights issues.

We create a platform where students can engage in meaningful discussions, campaigns, and initiatives contributing to social justice and equality.

Our Mission

  • Promote awareness of human rights issues
  • Encourage student participation in advocacy
  • Organize educational events and workshops
  • Collaborate with national and international organizations
