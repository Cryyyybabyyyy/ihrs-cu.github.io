About IHRS

The International Human Rights Society (IHRS) is a student-led community dedicated to promoting awareness, education, and advocacy for human rights at local, national, and global levels.

We aim to create a platform where students can engage in meaningful discussions, campaigns, and initiatives that contribute to social justice and equality.

Our Mission

  • Promote awareness of human rights issues
  • Encourage student participation in advocacy
  • Organize educational events and discussions
  • Collaborate with national and international organizations
