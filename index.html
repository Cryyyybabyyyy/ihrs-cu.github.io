Our Mission

The International Human Rights Society (IHRS) aims to foster an inclusive space for discussion, learning, and action on human rights issues. We believe that informed communities are the foundation of meaningful change.

What We Do

• Awareness campaigns and discussions
• Educational talks and workshops
• Collaborations with NGOs and student groups
• Advocacy and community engagement initiatives

Get Involved

Whether you are a student, researcher, or ally, IHRS welcomes everyone interested in promoting and protecting human rights.
